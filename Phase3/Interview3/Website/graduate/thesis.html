Thesis Workshops


The Graduate School makes thesis workshops available to help students understand and implement required formatting within your thesis. These workshops are designed to help with the thesis process.
Students will learn how to format page numbers and margins, utilize the templates and submit their thesis through ProQuest for approval.
Thesis Workshops will be held at the beginning of the fall and spring semesters. You should attend a workshop before you begin writing your thesis.

Registration is required to attend


To register for the thesis workshop, or if you have any questions, please contact the Graduate School at graduateschool@siue.edu or (618) 650-3010.
