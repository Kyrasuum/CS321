Students planning to major in Chemistry (ACS degree) should begin their Math and Chemistry courses as soon as possible in the freshman year in order to complete the degree in four years.
Recommended “out of department” electives (choose according to interests and career goals):
Math: Calculus 3, Differential Equations, Engineering Mathematics, Statistics (Math 480)
Biology: Cell & Molecular, Genetics, Immunology, Microbiology, Recombinant DNA, BIO 431
Physics: Modern Physics, Optics, Light & Color
Computer Science: 150,151,404
Other: Scientific Drawing, Economics, Accounting, GBA 300, Management, Environmental Geochemistry, Graphical Information Systems, Finance, Foreign Languages, History of Chemistry, more Communications
